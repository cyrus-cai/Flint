We are excited to announce the release of **HyperNote v0.9**! This update brings significant improvements to our subscription system, powerful new AI features, and numerous workflow enhancements.

## 🌟 Subscription System

We've overhauled the subscription experience to make it more transparent and user-friendly:

- **Improved Status Display**: The `AboutSettingsView` now provides a clearer, more detailed view of your current subscription status.
- **Localization**: Added full Chinese support for all subscription-related features.
- **Robust Validation**: Refactored the underlying logic to ensure faster and more accurate subscription verification.

## 🤖 AI Features

Your notes are getting smarter:

- **AI Summaries**: The notification system now includes AI-generated summaries, giving you a quick gist of your alerts.
- **Auto-Title Generation**: HyperNote can now automatically generate titles for your notes based on their content (via DoubaoAPI).

## ⚡ Workflow Enhancements

Efficiency is key:

- **Quick Access**: Triple-tap the `Option` key to instantly access your recent notes.
- **Clipboard Monitoring**: Enhanced clipboard handling with better event monitoring and new user settings.
- **Note Deletion**: You can now delete notes directly from the interface, with visual cues in the settings (destructive actions are now highlighted).

## 🛠️ Polish & Fixes

- **macOS Compatibility**: Refactored `MainWindowController` for better compatibility with upcoming macOS releases.
- **UI Responsiveness**: Updated `ContentView` background handling for a smoother visual experience.
- **Project Cleanup**: Removed legacy user-specific Xcode project files for a cleaner codebase.
